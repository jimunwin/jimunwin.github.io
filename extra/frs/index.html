FRS - Fuel Recovery Services

Here at FRS, in association with sister companies and business partners, we can solve the problem of in-tank and on tanker fuel crossovers with speed, efficiency and service. We are registered oil waste carriers, fully complying with the latest environmental regulations and are able to handle any fuels including petrol.
Everyone in the operations side of FRS are fully trained in fuel handling and operate to the highest standards on safety and environmental protection.
Our vehicles can carry up to 38,000 litres and using our specialised equipment we are capable of uplifting product from depth and distance.
We operate a round the clock service so just call us for a cost estimate for the recovery and to agree a time to uplift the product.
